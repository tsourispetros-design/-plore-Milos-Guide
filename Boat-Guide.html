SCENIC PASS-BY Suggested Route

ROUTE STOP 01

A beautiful coastal route designed for a smooth and unforgettable Milos experience. Take your time, enjoy the scenery, and cruise responsibly.

Leaving Agios Sostis, cruise east along the coastline toward Firiplaka. Continue past Tsigrado and after Gerakas, enjoying the crystal-clear waters and unique volcanic scenery. After that, cruise west, passing Agios Sostis on your way to Gerontas and the famous Kleftiko caves for the final part of the journey.

Enjoy each stop at a relaxed cruising pace
Avoid crowded swimming areas during peak hours
Keep a comfortable distance from rocky shores

Explore with care

Enjoy the caves and arches from a safe distance. Do not force the boat into narrow places. Swim and snorkel only when the boat is safely anchored and conditions are calm.

SAFETY ESSENTIALS

Simple rules

These small rules keep the day relaxed, safe and enjoyable.

Stay at least 100 meters away from rocks and shallow reefs.
Drive slowly near beaches, swimmers and other boats.
Never leave the boat before checking the anchor.
Do not enter narrow caves or tight rock passages with the boat.
If wind or sea changes, choose the safer option and return earlier.
If anything happens, drop anchor if possible and contact us on WhatsApp.

BEFORE DEPARTURE
